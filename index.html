Our service

At Bean & Byte, we believe that coffee is more than just a caffeine kick its a meticulously engineered craft. Our beans are ethically sourced directly from the volcanic highlands of Indonesia, then roasted in small, controlled batches to ensure every cup achieves a precise equilibrium of acidity, body, and sweetness. Whether you are a fan of a bold, high-pressure Ristretto to fuel a debugging session or a creamy, micro-foamed Oat Milk Latte for a creative break, our baristas treat every single pour as a piece of functional art.
